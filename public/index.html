Who We Are

About Us

Light Bearers is a community-driven organization dedicated to equipping young people with the tools, values, and opportunities they need to grow spiritually, academically, and professionally. We believe in empowering the next generation to be leaders of integrity and purpose.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

We are a community-based organization focused on creating opportunities for high school and university students to thrive academically, socially, and economically.
